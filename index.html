Why Does College Matter?

On average, college graduates earn 30k more annually than those with high school diplomas. Although higher education is large financial strain and investment of time, it results in long-term financial stability, more career opportunities, and thus, long-term satisfaction.

Who We Are

As children of immigrants, we understand the importance of higher education. However, we also realize how intimidating and complicated it can be. Resources can range from free, with limited tools, to expensive camps and college counselors. We wanted to bring a tool which would allow students to organize and plan their route to college.
